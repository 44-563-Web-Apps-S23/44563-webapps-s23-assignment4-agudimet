Anuradha Gudimetla "Snips a Play"

SAM AVERAGE by PERCY MACKAYE
JOEL.

[_With a singing call._]

Four o'clock!--All's well!

[_Jumping down from the embankment, he approaches the fire._ANDREW.

By God, Joel, it's bitter.

JOEL.

[_Rubbing his hands over the coals._]

A mite sharpish.

ANDREW.

[_Looks up eagerly._]

What?

JOEL.

Cuts sharp, for Thanksgivin'.

ELLEN.

Nearly a year----

ANDREW.

Now, at last!

ELLEN.

Hold me close, Andy.

EMILY.

[_Quietly._]

Tell him, Mary; it's best now.
